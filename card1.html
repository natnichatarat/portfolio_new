Moody Application

A mobile application designed for daily emotion management, which uses emojis to visualize mood on a calendar interface. Users can also input notes to describe their feelings. Developed with Flutter.

Key Features:

  • Users must register and log in to their accounts
  • Choose an emoji to express your feelings for that day on your calendar.
  • Click on the emoji of the day will prompt you to write an additional note, explaining how you feel.
